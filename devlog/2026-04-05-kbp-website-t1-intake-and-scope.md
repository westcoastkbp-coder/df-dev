## Objective

Define the minimum approval-ready scope for the first West Coast KBP company website build. This task is intentionally optimized for execution discipline, not visual polish.

## Scope Summary

The website will be a small business-marketing site with four required outcomes:

1. Present the company clearly.
2. Explain available services.
3. Provide direct contact paths.
4. Capture leads through a simple inquiry form.

This phase defines what will be built in the first implementation pass and what will remain out of scope until later approval.

## Proposed Page Structure

1. Home
2. Contact

The Home page will carry the company presentation, services overview, trust signals, and lead capture entry point. The Contact page will provide direct communication details and a secondary lead capture path.

## Content Sections

### Home

1. Hero
Purpose: identify West Coast KBP, state core value proposition, and direct users to contact or request a quote.

2. Company Overview
Purpose: explain who the company is, what it does, and who it serves.

3. Services Overview
Purpose: list primary service categories with short descriptions.

4. Why Choose Us
Purpose: provide simple trust-building points such as responsiveness, reliability, and field execution discipline.

5. Lead Capture Call To Action
Purpose: invite users to request contact, estimate, or project discussion.

6. Footer
Purpose: provide repeated contact access and basic company identity.

### Contact

1. Contact Header
Purpose: confirm that the page is for direct outreach.

2. Contact Methods
Purpose: expose phone, email, and any service-area or operating-hour information available.

3. Lead Capture Form
Purpose: collect prospect details in a structured way.

4. Confirmation State
Purpose: show that the inquiry was received and that follow-up is expected.

## Basic Layout

### Global Layout

1. Simple top navigation with links to Home and Contact
2. Single-column mobile-first content flow
3. Standard section-based landing-page layout on desktop
4. Repeated primary call-to-action in hero, mid-page, and contact areas

### Layout Principles

1. Prioritize clarity over branding refinement
2. Keep sections shallow and easily scannable
3. Use clear headings and short supporting copy blocks
4. Make the lead form reachable without hunting

## Lead Capture Definition

Minimum required fields:

1. Name
2. Email or phone
3. Service needed
4. Message

Minimum required behavior:

1. Client-side validation for required fields
2. Clear submission action
3. Visible success confirmation after submission
4. Safe fallback if backend delivery is not yet wired

## Success Criteria

T1 is complete when all of the following are true:

1. The page structure is explicitly defined.
2. Each required business outcome maps to a page section.
3. The basic layout direction is defined well enough for implementation.
4. Lead capture requirements are concrete enough to build without additional coordination.
5. This document is reviewable as an approval gate for T2-WEBSITE-SCAFFOLD.

## Out Of Scope For T1

1. Final visual design system
2. Brand polish and advanced animation
3. SEO refinement
4. Analytics integration
5. CRM integration
6. Production deployment
7. Copywriting optimization beyond placeholder-ready structure
